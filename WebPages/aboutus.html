About Us

Our History

AlgoFlow is a South African NPO targeting young-minded individuals still in secondary school. We provide access to coding, algorithmic, and problem-solving skills through workshops aligned with tertiary NQF study levels. This helps learners get a glimpse of tertiary study materials, fostering interest in technology and computer science faculties.

Mission Statement

AlgoFlow prepares young secondary school learners for real-world technology challenges. We provide easy-to-follow courses that introduce students to tech concepts without affecting their current studies, sparking interest in future opportunities.

Vision Statement

Our vision is to nurture young minds interested in IT and technology-related studies. AlgoFlow enables learners to understand technology from a basic level through digital literacy and computational skills, preparing them for the potential workforce environment.
