## Project OverView
Algo flow is a South African NPO in targeted for Young Minded Individuals who are still doing secondary Study To be given the opportunity of having access to coding, Algorithmic and problem solving skills through Essential workshops Under Tertiary Study NQF levels. Algo Flow gives young Minds under Secondary Study to Have Glimpse at tertiary Study Learning Materials that will allow them to understand and may have interest in the faculties of technology and computer science. Algo Flow is Specifically Targeted For people aged 16-25 . Allowing for A Simple and Professional layout Interface for users.


### Mission and Vision
 Our Determination at Algo Flow is Prepare young Individual in Secondary Institution for the Outcomes of How the Real world operates in Technology after Matriculating High School By providing  Easy courses that will not affect their currents studies but gain an interest of what to expect Before or After Matriculating or Graduating from university.
Seek Potential in Young Minds who would like to study and gain degrees in It or Other technological based courses. It is giving the Opportunity to Understand Technology from how little we know about it through digital literacy  and computational skills. Algo Flows vision is assist young learners and prepare them for the Potential Workforce Environment.

### Goals & objectives
-	Awareness Use the website’s homepage and programs page to raise awareness about coding opportunities.
-	Engagement Create interactive elements (sign-up forms, embedded videos) to promote participation in mentorship programs.
-	Community Growth Showcase volunteers and mentors on the "About Us" and "Programs" sections to inspire involvement.
-	Education Pipeline Encourage learners to pursue tertiary technology courses by using the website as a stepping stone, with structured resources and links to further study.

-	Responsive Design: Develop a user interface that adapts seamlessly to different screen sizes (desktop, tablet, and mobile), making the website accessible to both high school learners and the public.
-	Clear and Engaging Layout: Use a clean, colourful, and structured design that highlights key information while maintaining readability and professionalism.
-	Integrated Learning Resources: Embed YouTube tutorials and coding content directly into the site, allowing secondary school learners to access simple coding basics without leaving the platform.


## Key Features and Functionality

#### Homepage
-	Features a large, engaging introduction image as the first section.
-	Includes a navigation header with clickable links to all other webpages.
-	Displays a welcome message in the second section, giving visitors an overview of Algo Flow.
-	The footer section contains essential information and social media icons for easy access.

#### About Us Webpage
-	Organized into three main sections for clarity and readability.
-	The first section provides a comprehensive explanation of Algo Flow’s purpose, goals, and audience.
-	Displays both vision and mission statements prominently.
-	Includes a “Meet the Team” section showcasing all board members, their roles, and photos.

#### Programs Webpage
-	Lists all available programs that users can explore and show interest in.
-	Features sponsor videos and images to highlight partnerships and support.

#### Mentor Webpage
-	Provides a registration form for users to sign up for mentorship and volunteer programs.
-	Collects personal details and inquiries, enabling the team to follow up with interested candidates.
-	Ensures mobile-friendly design for accessibility on different devices.

#### Contact Page
-	Shows a phone number and email address for inquiries or assistance.
-	Includes links to social media platforms for additional ways to connect and get information.
-	Displays interactive maps and visuals for location and navigation guidance.
